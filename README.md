# Environmental_Impact_Analysis_for_Holistic_Health_Revenue_Optimization
This project analyzed the relationship between environmental factors( especially pollen levels and weather conditions) and healthcare services demand for a holistic health clinic offering respiratory, immunity, and wellness services.A publicly available grass pollen dataset from kaggle is
combined with a synthetically generated clinical dataset to simulate patient
visits and revenue patterns over time.
To investigate if the spikes in the air pollution or pollen count have any
relation with the respiratory(allergy, athma) and immunity of people and to
justify the increasing budget during this time.
Overall, the analysis confirms that the environmental stressors are
reliable predictors of healthcare demand, and strategic seasonal
marketing and adjustments can improve patient acquisition efficiently
and revenue optimization.
